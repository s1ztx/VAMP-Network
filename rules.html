These rules are active — they apply to all VAMP Network platforms including the Minecraft server, Discord, and any future services.
v2.1
Ignorance is not an excuse. By participating in VAMP Network on any platform, you agree to follow these rules in full. Staff have the final say in all moderation decisions. Rules may be updated at any time — check back regularly and watch #announcements in Discord.
General Conduct
The foundation. These apply everywhere on VAMP Network.
7 rules
G-1
Treat everyone with respect
All players, staff, and visitors deserve to be treated with basic human dignity. Harassment, bullying, discrimination, or targeted hate based on race, gender, religion, nationality, sexual orientation, or disability is strictly prohibited — in any language or context.

No cheating or exploiting of any kind
Using hacked clients, cheat software, macro scripts, autoclickers, or exploiting any bug, glitch, or unintended mechanic to gain an advantage over other players is forbidden. This includes using external modifications that alter gameplay in any way not explicitly permitted by VAMP Network.

G-3
No sharing of personal information
Do not share or request the real name, address, phone number, social media, or any other personal information of yourself or other players. Doxxing — intentionally revealing someone's private information — will result in an immediate permanent ban with no appeal.

Chat Rules
Applies to in-game chat, Discord messages, and all VAMP communication channels.
6 rules
C-1
No hate speech or slurs
Racial slurs, homophobic language, sexist remarks, ableist language, or any form of derogatory speech targeting a person or group is never acceptable. Context does not excuse it. Even "joking" use of slurs is a violation.
MuteTemp BanPerm Ban
Severity
C-2
No spamming or flooding
Sending the same or similar messages repeatedly, excessive use of CAPS LOCK, character spam (e.g. "aaaaaaa"), or any behavior that makes chat unusable for others is prohibited. This includes automated spam from scripts or bots.
WarningMute
Severity
C-3
No threats or intimidation
Threatening other players with in-game harm, doxxing threats, DDoS threats, or any statement intended to make someone feel unsafe is a serious violation. Threats are treated as real regardless of whether you intended to follow through.
Permanent Ban
Severity
C-4
Keep it age-appropriate
VAMP Network is played by people of all ages. Explicit sexual content, graphic violence in descriptions, or any content that would be considered NSFW is not permitted in any public chat. Keep discussions appropriate for a general audience at all times.
WarningMuteTemp Ban
Severity
C-5
No false accusations against players or staff
Falsely accusing another player or staff member of hacking, cheating, or rule violations with no evidence — especially in public chat — undermines the community. If you have a genuine report, use #player-reports in Discord with evidence.
WarningMute
Severity
C-6
English in global chat
Global and public chat channels must be used in English so staff can moderate effectively. Private messages and party chats may use any language. If you need support in another language, let staff know in Discord and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Gameplay Rules
Rules that maintain fair and enjoyable gameplay for everyone.
6 rules
GP-1
No griefing outside designated PvP zones
Destroying, damaging, modifying, or stealing from another player's build, farm, or belongings without their explicit consent is griefing. This includes lava casting, flooding, and using TNT near player builds. Griefed items will be restored where possible and the griefer will be banned.
Temp BanPerm Ban if Severe
Severity
GP-2
No stealing from other players
Taking items from another player's unlocked chests, farms, or dropped items that clearly belong to someone else is stealing and is not permitted. "It wasn't locked" is not a valid defense. If you find an unprotected chest, report it to staff rather than taking the contents.
WarningTemp Ban
Severity
GP-3
No AFK farming beyond reasonable limits
Automated AFK farming using macros, auto-clickers, or weight-on-key methods that allow indefinite resource accumulation while you're away from the keyboard is not permitted. Standard mob farms are fine. If you're AFK and gaining resources continuously, that's a violation.
WarningKick
Severity
GP-4
No trapping or luring players unfairly
Building traps designed to kill players outside of designated PvP areas, or luring players to their deaths through deception, is prohibited. This includes fake portals, pit traps disguised as paths, and any mechanism designed to kill unsuspecting players.
WarningTemp Ban
Severity
GP-5
No market manipulation or scamming
Deliberately misleading other players in trades, misrepresenting item quality or quantity, bait-and-switch trades, and any form of in-game economic manipulation or scamming is prohibited. All trades are final once completed — staff will not reverse fair trades.
WarningTemp BanPerm if Repeated
Severity
GP-6
No lag machines or server disruption
Intentionally building structures designed to cause server lag — including excessive entity farms, recursive redstone loops, TNT cannons firing continuously, and similar constructions — will result in an immediate removal of the build and a ban.
Temp BanPerm Ban if Intentional
Severity
PvP Rules
Combat is part of the experience — but it comes with boundaries.
5 rules
PV-1
PvP only in designated zones
Player vs. player combat is only permitted in clearly marked PvP zones. Attacking players outside of these areas — including spawn, market districts, and peaceful zones — is strictly prohibited. Zone boundaries are enforced by the server and by staff.
WarningTemp Ban
Severity
PV-2
No spawn killing or camping
Repeatedly killing a player immediately after they respawn, or camping a spawn point to prevent a player from playing, is toxic and prohibited. Give players a fair chance to orient themselves and move away from spawn before engaging in combat.
WarningKickTemp Ban
Severity
PV-3
No teaming in solo modes
In solo game modes, coordinating or working with other players who are supposed to be opponents gives an unfair advantage. Teaming — including truce agreements, refusing to fight allies, or sharing information with opponents — is prohibited in solo modes.
WarningTemp Ban
Severity
PV-4
No combat logging
Disconnecting from the server to avoid death during active combat — also known as "combat logging" — is prohibited and will be treated as if you died in combat, with any applicable penalties applied. The server logs all disconnections during combat.
WarningTemp Ban
Severity
PV-5
Trash talk must stay sportsmanlike
Light competitive banter is part of gaming culture and is allowed. However, personal insults, slurs, or sustained targeted harassment following a match cross the line and are a violation of G-1. Winning doesn't license you to be abusive in victory.
WarningMute
Severity
Building Rules
Keep the world looking great for everyone.
4 rules
B-1
No inappropriate structures
Building structures that are offensive, sexual, or designed to harass (e.g. hate symbols, genitalia, targeted build harassment) is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate removal of the build and a ban. Staff have the authority to remove any build deemed inappropriate without warning.
Temp BanPerm Ban if Targeted
Severity
B-2
Respect plot and claim boundaries
Do not build directly adjacent to another player's claim in a way that blocks their expansion, cuts off their access, or intentionally disrupts their build. Maintain a reasonable distance — at least 20 blocks — from any claimed land you don't own unless you have permission.
WarningBuild Removal
Severity
B-3
No 1×1 towers or floating structures in shared zones
Leaving unfinished, unsightly, or abandoned structures in shared spaces — including 1×1 dirt towers, floating trees, and incomplete builds — is not allowed. You are responsible for cleaning up any temporary structures you make. Abandoned builds may be removed by staff.

Farms must be contained
All mob farms, animal farms, and item farms must be fully enclosed and contained within your claimed land. Animals, mobs, or items escaping your farm into shared or other players' territory is your responsibility. Large farms require staff approval — submit a request in Discord.

Staff Interactions
How to work with, and what to expect from, the VAMP staff team.
4 rules
S-1
Follow staff instructions
When a staff member issues an instruction, follow it. If you believe the instruction is unfair, follow it first and then raise a formal complaint through the proper channels. Refusing to comply with staff, arguing excessively in public, or being disrespectful to staff will escalate your punishment.
WarningKickTemp Ban
Severity
S-2
Do not abuse the report system
Filing false or malicious reports against other players wastes staff time and undermines the system for genuine victims. If you file a report and it is found to be deliberately false, you will receive a punishment equal to what the accused would have received had the report been genuine.
WarningTemp Ban
Severity
S-3
Staff decisions are final in-server
Staff decisions during active moderation are final in the moment. The correct path to challenge a decision is through a formal appeal or complaint — not by arguing in chat, DMing every staff member, or posting about it publicly. Appeals are reviewed by Senior Staff and are taken seriously.
Appeal via Discord
Severity
S-4
Do not bribe or attempt to influence staff
Offering in-game items, real money, or any other incentive to staff members in exchange for preferential treatment, overlooking a violation, or unfair advantages is corruption and will result in a permanent ban. Staff are expected to refuse all such offers and report them immediately.
Permanent Ban
Severity
Punishments & Escalation
How consequences are applied, escalated, and appealed.
3 rules
P-1
Punishments escalate with repeated offences
Most punishments follow a graduated system: Warning → Mute → Kick → Temp Ban → Permanent Ban. Each repeated violation of the same rule moves you one step up the ladder. Severe violations (e.g. doxxing, cheating, hate speech) skip directly to the highest tier.
Policy
P-2
Punishment evasion doubles the sentence
Using an alternate Minecraft account or Discord account to bypass a ban, mute, or any active punishment is considered punishment evasion. Both accounts will be permanently banned with no appeal window. The original punishment's duration is also doubled upon discovery of evasion.
Permanent Ban
Severity
P-3
All punishments are logged and reviewable
Every warning, mute, kick, and ban is logged with a timestamp, reason, and the staff member who issued it. This ensures accountability on both sides. Punishment logs may be referenced in appeals. Any staff member who misuses their moderation powers is subject to demotion.
Transparency PolicyAppeals Welcome
