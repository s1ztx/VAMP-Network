About Us

Built Different.
From Day One.

VAMP Network is not another Minecraft server. We are building a carefully crafted gaming experience from the ground up — where every system, every feature, and every moment of gameplay has been designed with intention.

Custom-Built Infrastructure
Our servers run on optimized hardware with custom software stacks designed for maximum throughput and minimum latency across all game modes.
Community at the Core
Every feature decision is driven by player feedback. Our community shapes the network through active participation in Discord and voting systems.
Original Development
Zero cookie-cutter plugins. Every system from anti-cheat to economy is developed in-house, giving us complete control over the player experience.
Competitive Excellence
Ranked systems, seasonal events, and leaderboards give competitive players the depth they demand while welcoming casual players too.

Premium / Pay-to-Win Policy
When is VAMP Network launching?
We don't have a confirmed launch date yet. Our priority is getting everything right rather than rushing to launch. We're currently in Phase 3 of development, working on core systems. Join our Discord to be the first to know when a date is announced.
What Minecraft version will VAMP Network support?
We will support the latest stable version of Minecraft Java Edition at launch. Multi-version support (allowing older clients) is being evaluated. Bedrock Edition support is on our long-term roadmap but won't be available at initial launch.
Is VAMP Network pay-to-win?
Absolutely not. This is a core principle of VAMP Network. Any purchasable items in our store will be purely cosmetic — custom prefixes, visual effects, cosmetic items, and quality-of-life perks that don't affect gameplay balance. Competitive integrity is non-negotiable for us.
How can I apply for a staff position?
Staff applications are not currently open. When they open, all information will be posted in our Discord server. The best way to prepare is to be an active, positive member of our community — that's what we look for first. We'll never accept unsolicited DM applications.
What game modes will be available at launch?
We're finalizing the launch lineup, but core game modes will include competitive PvP, survival with enhanced mechanics, and at least one original custom game mode unique to VAMP Network. The full announcement will be made closer to beta testing.
How do I join the beta test?
Beta tester spots will be allocated to active, established Discord members first. Being engaged in the community, helping others, and providing constructive feedback in our development discussions is the best way to improve your chances. Applications will be announced well in advance.
Will the server be available globally?
Yes. VAMP Network will be accessible to players worldwide. Our infrastructure is being designed with international players in mind, with server locations optimized to minimize latency for the largest possible player base. Regional details will be shared during the infrastructure phase.
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
All bug reports and feature suggestions go through our Discord server. We have dedicated channels for each — your feedback is taken seriously and directly reviewed by the development team. Good suggestions are tracked and credited to the community member who submitted them.
